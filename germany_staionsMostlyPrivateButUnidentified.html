E-HDT Charger Information

This map shows Electric Heavy-Duty Transport (E-HDT) charging stations across Germany.

E-HDT chargers typically have:

  • High power (≥150kW)
  • High voltage (≥500V)
  • CCS or high-power Type 2 connectors

Click on markers for detailed information and to see charger IDs for route planning.

Note: Data is static from OpenStreetMap. Real-time availability requires e-MSP services.
